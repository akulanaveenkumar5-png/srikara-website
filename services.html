Services

Srikara Financial Services supports professional dialogue between project sponsors and investors exploring structured capital opportunities.

Advisory Areas

Structured Capital Dialogue

Supporting discussions around structured capital opportunities between project sponsors and potential investors.

Investor Introductions

Where relevant, project opportunities may be introduced to investors exploring capital deployment opportunities.

Project Engagement Guidance

Providing clarity on how project sponsors may initiate early-stage capital discussions in a responsible and structured manner.

Capital Structure Discussions

Exploring potential capital structures suitable for project development conversations.

NRI Capital Dialogue

Facilitating conversations related to potential NRI participation in structured capital opportunities.

Strategic Financial Dialogue

Encouraging professional communication between stakeholders exploring long-term capital partnerships.

Service Principles

Transparency

Promoting open and responsible financial dialogue between project sponsors and investors.

Independent Evaluation

All investment decisions remain subject to independent evaluation and due diligence by investors.

Professional Engagement

Supporting ethical and long-term professional collaboration between stakeholders.

Important Clarification

Srikara Financial Services operates as an independent platform facilitating dialogue between project sponsors and investors.

The firm does not operate as a bank, lender or investment fund and does not guarantee financing outcomes.

All investment decisions remain subject to independent evaluation and due diligence by investors.
