Engagement Terms

These engagement terms outline the general framework under which professional discussions may occur through Srikara Financial Services.

Srikara Financial Services operates as an independent advisory and coordination platform supporting dialogue between project sponsors, investors and stakeholders exploring capital opportunities.

The platform may assist in facilitating introductions, communication and structured discussions where appropriate.

Any financing discussions remain subject to independent evaluation, due diligence and decision making by the respective stakeholders involved.

Srikara Financial Services shall not be held responsible for investment outcomes, project performance, lending approvals or commercial agreements between parties.

Acceptance of Terms

By initiating communication or engagement through this platform, users acknowledge and agree to these general engagement principles.
