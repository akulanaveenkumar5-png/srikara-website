Project Engagement

Srikara Financial Services facilitates structured dialogue between project sponsors and investors exploring capital opportunities.

Engagement Process

Initial Project Discussion

Project sponsors may initiate confidential discussions by sharing high-level information about their project including sector, capital requirements and development stage.

Preliminary Evaluation

Early discussions may help determine whether the project concept aligns with potential investor interest.

Investor Dialogue

Where relevant, project opportunities may be introduced to investors exploring structured capital opportunities.

Independent Decision

All investment decisions remain subject to independent evaluation, due diligence and approval by investors.

Engagement Philosophy

Srikara Financial Services emphasizes transparent financial dialogue and responsible engagement between stakeholders exploring capital opportunities.

The objective is to support professional communication between project sponsors and investors while respecting applicable regulatory and compliance frameworks.

Important Clarification

Srikara Financial Services operates as an independent platform supporting dialogue between project sponsors and investors.

The firm does not operate as a bank, lender, NBFC or investment fund and does not guarantee financing outcomes.

All investment decisions remain subject to independent evaluation and due diligence by investors.
